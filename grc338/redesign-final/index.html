How You Can Help

Donate

As a small group of volunteers, we are always thankful for any donations, whether in the form of monetary donations or donated food and supplies. If you would like to make a donation, please send us an email or give us a call. Our contact info can be found on the contact us page.

Volunteer

Our team is made up of volunteers dedicated to helping chinchillas in need. If you are interested in volunteering, visit the contact us page and send a resume to our email.

Surrender a Chinchilla

If you need to surrender a chinchilla, or know of a chinchilla in need of re-homing, please contact us through email or phone or in the case of an emergency, call your vet.
